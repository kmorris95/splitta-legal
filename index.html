Getting Started

How do I create a group?

Tap the "+" button on your dashboard, select "New Group", name it, and invite friends via link or email. It's that easy!

Can I split bills unequally?

Yes! Choose between equal splits, custom amounts, or percentages. Perfect for couples or roommates with different incomes.

How do recurring bills work?

Set any bill to repeat monthly. Splitta automatically creates new bills on your chosen date - never forget Netflix again!

Can I use Splitta offline?

Yes! Create and edit bills offline. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.

Troubleshooting

  • Can't log in? Try resetting your password or check your internet connection
  • Missing bills? Pull down to refresh and ensure you're in the right group
  • Invite link not working? Links expire after 7 days - create a new one
  • Payment not updating? Allow a few seconds for sync, or manually refresh
